Tomorrow's forecast, a tale to tell,
In Aalborg, chilly, with winds that swell.
In Madrid, rain falls, with skies so grey,
But Úbeda's warmth, is where I'd stay.

Mañana, el pronóstico, una historia que contar,
En Aalborg, frío, con vientos que soplan fuerte.
En Madrid, llueve, con cielos grises,
Pero el calor de Úbeda, es donde me gustaría estar.

(Note: I've taken some creative liberties to make the poem flow better, while still conveying the main idea of comparing the weather in the three locations and suggesting that Úbeda might be the nicest place to be tomorrow, despite the rain.)
