Here's a short bilingual poem comparing the weather in Aalborg, Madrid, and Úbeda:

Tomorrow's forecast, let's see,
Mañana, el pronóstico, veamos,
Aalborg's chill, with winds so strong,
Aalborg fresco, con vientos fuertes,
Madrid's warm, with sunshine bright,
Madrid cálido, con sol radiante,
Úbeda's mild, a pleasant sight,
Úbeda suave, un espectáculo agradable.

If I had to choose, I'd say,
Si tuviera que elegir, diría,
Úbeda's the place, to be tomorrow,
Úbeda es el lugar, para mañana,
With gentle breeze, and warm delight,
Con brisa suave, y calor agradable,
It's the perfect spot, to enjoy the light,
Es el lugar perfecto, para disfrutar la luz.
