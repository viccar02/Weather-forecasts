Here's a short bilingual poem comparing the weather in Aalborg, Madrid, and Úbeda:

English:
Aalborg's chill begins to fade,
Madrid's warmth is nicely made,
Úbeda's sun shines bright and bold,
Where would you rather be tomorrow, cold?
Madrid's gentle breeze is a delight,
Úbeda's heat is a warm, sunny sight,
Aalborg's winds are strong and free,
But Madrid's charm is where I'd be.

Spanish:
El frío de Aalborg comienza a desvanecerse,
El calor de Madrid es agradable y se hace,
El sol de Úbeda brilla con fuerza y valor,
¿Dónde preferirías estar mañana, con frío o calor?
La brisa suave de Madrid es un deleite,
El calor de Úbeda es una vista cálida y soleada,
Los vientos de Aalborg son fuertes y libres,
Pero el encanto de Madrid es donde me gustaría estar.

Note: This poem is a creative comparison of the weather in the three locations, and it suggests that Madrid would be the nicest place to be tomorrow due to its pleasant warmth and gentle breeze.
