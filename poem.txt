Here's a short bilingual poem comparing the weather in Aalborg, Madrid, and Úbeda:

English:
Aalborg's chill begins to bite,
Madrid's warmth shines with delight.
Úbeda's sun shines bright and bold,
Tomorrow, Madrid's warmth to hold.

Spanish:
El frío de Aalborg comienza a morder,
El calor de Madrid brilla con encanto.
El sol de Úbeda brilla fuerte y valiente,
Mañana, el calor de Madrid es donde estar.

Note: The poem suggests that Madrid would be the nicest place to be tomorrow, given its warmer temperatures compared to Aalborg and Úbeda.
