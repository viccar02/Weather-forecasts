Aalborg, Madrid, and Úbeda, three cities so fair,
Compare their weather, with differences to share.
Aalborg's chill, with winds that do blow,
Madrid's warm, with sunshine to glow.
Úbeda's mild, with temperatures just right,
A pleasant place to be, on a sunny night.

Aalborg, frío, con vientos que soplan,
Madrid, cálido, con sol que brilla y plan,
Úbeda, suave, con temperaturas justas,
Un lugar agradable, en una noche soleada y clara.

Tomorrow, where would you like to be?
Perhaps Úbeda, with its gentle breeze,
Or Madrid, with its warm sunshine spree,
Aalborg, though chilly, still has its charm to see.
But if I had to choose, I'd say Úbeda's the place,
With its mild weather, and a warm, sunny face.

Mañana, ¿dónde te gustaría estar?
Quizás Úbeda, con su brisa suave,
O Madrid, con su cálida fiesta de sol,
Aalborg, aunque frío, todavía tiene su encanto que ver.
Pero si tuviera que elegir, diría que Úbeda es el lugar,
Con su clima suave, y una cara cálida y soleada.
