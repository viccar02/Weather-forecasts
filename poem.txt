Aalborg, Madrid, and Úbeda, three cities apart,
Compare their weather, a different start.
Aalborg's chill, with winds so strong,
Madrid's warm, with sunshine all day long.
Úbeda's mild, with temperatures just right,
A pleasant place, for a spring delight.

Mañana, tomorrow, where to be?
Perhaps Úbeda, with its gentle breeze.
No lluvia, no rain, just sun and fun,
A perfect day, for everyone.

Madrid está cálido, con sol durante todo el día.
Úbeda está suave, con temperaturas justas,
Un lugar agradable, para un deleite primaveral.

Mañana, ¿dónde estar?
Quizás Úbeda, con su brisa suave.
