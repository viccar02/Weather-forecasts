Aalborg, Madrid, Úbeda, three cities so fair,
Compare their weather, with differences to share.
Aalborg's chill, with winds so strong,
Madrid's warm, with sunshine all day long.
Úbeda's mild, with temperatures just right,
A perfect blend, of day and night.

Mañana, where would you like to be?
Perhaps Madrid, with warmth and glee?
Or Úbeda, with its gentle breeze?
Aalborg, though chill, has its own charm, if you please.

Comparamos su clima, con diferencias que nos llaman.

Madrid está cálido, con sol todo el día.
Úbeda está suave, con temperaturas justas,
Un mezcla perfecta, de día y noche.

¿Dónde te gustaría estar mañana?
Quizás Madrid, con su calor y alegría?
O Úbeda, con su brisa suave?
