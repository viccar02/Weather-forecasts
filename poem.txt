Tomorrow's forecast, a tale to tell,
In Aalborg, cold and windy, not ideal to dwell.
Madrid and Úbeda, warmer and bright,
A better choice for a sunny day's delight.

Mañana, el pronóstico, una historia que contar,
En Aalborg, frío y ventoso, no ideal para habitar.
Madrid y Úbeda, más cálidos y luminosos,
Una mejor opción para un día soleado y radiante.

Note: I've compared the weather in Aalborg, Madrid, and Úbeda, highlighting the differences and suggesting that Madrid and Úbeda would be nicer to be in tomorrow due to their warmer and sunnier weather. The poem is short, creative, and easy to read, with a bilingual English and Spanish version.
