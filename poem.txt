Tomorrow's forecast, a tale of three cities so fine,
Aalborg, Madrid, and Úbeda, with weather that does shine.
Aalborg's chill, with winds that do blow,
Madrid's warm, with sunshine that glows.
Úbeda's mild, with a gentle breeze that sway,
A perfect day, to be there, come what may.

Mañana, el pronóstico, una historia de tres ciudades tan finas,
Aalborg, Madrid y Úbeda, con un clima que brilla.
Aalborg está frío, con vientos que soplan,
Madrid está cálido, con sol que brilla.
Úbeda está suave, con una brisa que se balancea,
Un día perfecto, para estar allí, pase lo que pase.

Note: I've taken a poetic license to create a short and creative poem, while still trying to convey the differences in weather between the three cities. The poem suggests that Úbeda might be the nicest place to be tomorrow, due to its mild and gentle weather.
